Orpheus Game

Orpheus is a first person, procedurally generated dungeon crawler. It is made using Unity and C#.

Orpheus is being developed by a small group of friends to work on their portfolios and also just for a bit of fun. It is currently incomplete. We currently plan to add a lot more content and polish to the game in the coming months.

Check out a demo video here!

How To Play

  1. Click here to download v1.0 of the game.
  2. Extract Orpheus.zip to a folder.
  3. Run the Orpheus.exe file you extracted.
  4. Have fun and leave us feedback in our Google form.
